Your contribution contains personal data

Similarly, your photo can not represent a situation derogatory for the professional without his responsibility being clearly established. Also, your photo must show that it has been taken home, for example with a view of the entrance or the interior of his establishment, the course of his performance ...

    With regard to the activities related to human health, your contribution must relate to the general reception that has been reserved to you by the practitioner, without being able to include judgment on the competence even of the practitioner or on the medical act in itself

    Whether current or future court proceedings, appeals or court decisions, your opinion should not relay this information.

    Your contribution is illicit Antisemitism, homophobia, pornography, xenophobia, racism, discriminatory, referring to drugs etc., are illegal and can not be published.

    Similarly for photos showing explicitly cigarettes, firearms, alcohol, drugs ...

    Your contribution contains medical terms

    Medical terms referring to a drug name, pathology or disease, of a diet can not be published. Avoid words or phrases such as "back pain", "scoliosis" ...
